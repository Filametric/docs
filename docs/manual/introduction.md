# Assembly Manual

Welcome to the DryBase assembly manual. This guide will walk you through the complete assembly of your DryBase filament dryer, from unpacking to your first drying session.

!!! warning "Important Safety Information"
    The DryBase operates at temperatures up to 100°C and uses mains electricity. Read all safety instructions before beginning assembly. If you observe smoke, unusual odors, or unexpected heat during use, immediately disconnect from mains power and contact [contact@filametric.com](mailto:contact@filametric.com).

## Before You Begin

- **Read the entire manual** before starting assembly.
- **Verify your kit contents** against the parts list for your kit variant (Complete Kit or Core Kit).
- **Print all required parts** if you have the Core Kit, or the housing if you have the Complete Kit. See [Chapter 2](chapter-2-3d-printing.md) for print settings.
- **Prepare your workspace** — you'll need a clean, well-lit surface with enough room to lay out all components.
