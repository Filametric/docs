## Step 2 — Install heat-set inserts

<div class="assembly-step" markdown>
<div markdown>

**You will need:**

- Soldering iron with heat-set insert tip
- 4x M3 heat-set inserts (from Step 1)
- 1x Housing base (from Step 1)

<!-- TODO: Replace with your actual image -->
<!-- ![Installing heat-set inserts](../images/ch3-step2-inserts.jpg){ .step-image } -->

</div>
<div markdown>

1. Heat your soldering iron to **220°C**.
2. Place a heat-set insert on each of the four mounting holes.
3. Press the soldering iron tip onto the insert and push straight down.
4. Let the heat do the work — do not apply excessive force.
5. The insert should sit flush with or slightly below the surface.

!!! warning "Burn hazard"
    The soldering iron and freshly installed inserts are extremely hot. Allow parts to cool before handling.

!!! note "Tip"
    If an insert goes in crooked, reheat and straighten it while the plastic is still soft.
